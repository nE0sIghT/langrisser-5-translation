# Prologue poem (title attract loop), IMG.DAT asset 12, image 0 (768x252, 8bpp).
# Blank lines mark the four logical poem blocks. The build renders every line on
# one uniform pitch into a single continuous scroll strip (no gap between
# screens), then slices it into the three stored panels; a line may straddle a
# panel boundary and rejoins seamlessly as the engine scrolls.
The crimson elder sister,
a merciless queen,
looks down upon us in majesty.
The azure younger sister,
a merciless warrior,
looks coldly down upon us.
The skyfaring ship, reaper's wings,
carries our souls to hell.
We, the oppressed, look up
and glare at heaven in wrath.

Crimson elder, azure younger,
stand side by side in this night sky;
we find sorrow's color.
We lament, grieve, rage, laugh.
At last we clench our fists;
at last we nock our arrows.
We shall stand against
the merciless sisters;
that alone is the way to live.

Our wrath shall burn
this sky and all the earth;
our sorrow shall freeze
this sky and all the seas.
At last the merciless queen
is driven from this land,
and sets forth on a journey.
And the merciless younger sister
sleeps quietly,
waiting for her elder sister.

Leaving words of hatred,
the queen hides herself
in a frozen world of stars.
After more than two centuries,
she will return to this land
and sow seeds of misfortune,
brandishing the sword of madness
to seize all of this world.
