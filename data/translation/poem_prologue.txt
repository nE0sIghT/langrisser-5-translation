# Prologue poem (title attract loop), IMG.DAT asset 12, image 0 (768x252, 8bpp).
# Three columns separated by a line containing only "---", matching the
# original Japanese three-column layout. Blank lines are kept as spacing.
The crimson sister,
a merciless queen,
looks down in majesty.
The azure sister,
a merciless warrior,
looks down coldly.
The skyfaring ship,
the reaper's wings,
carries our souls to hell.
We, the oppressed, look up
and glare at heaven in wrath.
The crimson and azure sisters,
side by side in the night sky,
---
find the hue of sorrow.
We cry, grieve, rage, laugh;
at last we clench our fists,
at last we nock our arrows,
and rise against
the merciless sisters;
that alone is the way to live.
Our wrath shall burn
this sky and all the earth;
our sorrow shall freeze
this sky and all the seas.
At last the merciless queen
is driven from this land,
---
cast into exile.
The merciless younger sister
sleeps quietly, waiting
for her elder sister.
Leaving words of hatred,
the vanished queen
hides herself away
in a frozen world of stars.
After more than two centuries,
she will return to this land
and sow seeds of misfortune,
wielding the sword of madness
to seize all the world.
