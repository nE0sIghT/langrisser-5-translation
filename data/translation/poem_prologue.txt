# Prologue poem (title attract loop), IMG.DAT asset 12, image 0 (768x252, 8bpp).
# Three columns separated by a line containing only "---", matching the
# original Japanese three-column layout. Blank lines are kept as spacing.
The crimson sister,
a merciless queen,
in majesty looks down on us.
The azure sister,
a merciless warrior,
coldly looks down on us.
The sky-borne ship,
the reaper's wings,
bears our souls to hell.
We who look up are oppressed;
in wrath we glare at heaven.
---
Grief, sorrow,
anger and laughter —
at last we clench our fists
and rise against
the merciless sisters;
that alone is the way to live.
Our wrath shall burn
this sky and all the earth;
our sorrow shall drown
this sky and all the seas.
The merciless queen is
driven out, cast into exile,
---
sleeping quietly,
even as sisters.
Leaving words of hatred,
into a frozen world of stars
the vanished queen,
after two hundred years,
will return to this land
and sow seeds of misery.
Brandishing the sword
of madness,
to seize all the world.
